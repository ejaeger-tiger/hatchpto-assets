Family Tech Hub

Comprehensive guides to help Hatch Elementary families navigate technology safely and confidently

Empowering Our Tiger Cubs in the Digital World

In today's connected world, digital safety is just as important as teaching our children to look both ways before crossing the street. The Hatch PTO has created four comprehensive guides to help families make informed decisions and stay safe online.

Whether you're considering your child's first phone, strengthening your family's cyber security, or teaching digital safety skills, these resources are here to help.

First Phone Guide

A practical roadmap to help families decide if their child is ready for a phone - and how to set it up safely with clear rules, healthy boundaries, and the right parental controls.

  • Readiness checklist (age matters less than maturity)
  • Phone options + a progressive "earn access" approach
  • iPhone Screen Time + Android Family Link setup
  • Family phone contract + expectations
  • Red flags to watch + responsible use coaching
View the First Phone Guide

Cyber Security Guide

A practical family guide to securing your accounts, spotting scams, and protecting your identity - without needing to be a tech expert.

  • Strong passwords + the passphrase method
  • Password managers (Bitwarden / 1Password)
  • 2FA setup (best options + priority accounts)
  • Phishing & scam red flags to watch for
  • Device + Wi-Fi security best practices
View Cyber Security Guide

Digital Safety Guide

A practical, step-by-step guide to protect your kids online at home using DNS filtering, parental controls, and safer settings for YouTube, Roblox, and devices.

  • Cloudflare "1.1.1.1 for Families" DNS protection
  • Router-based parental controls & bedtime schedules
  • YouTube Kids + supervised YouTube settings
  • Roblox safety controls & chat restrictions
  • Google Family Link + Apple Screen Time setup
View Digital Safety Guide

Social Media Guide

A modern parent guide to social media: when kids are ready, which platforms matter most, how to turn on the right safety settings, and what warning signs to watch for.

  • Readiness checklist: when (and if) to start
  • Popular platforms explained (TikTok, Instagram, Snapchat, Discord)
  • Safety settings: privacy, messaging, and content controls
  • Family agreement + boundaries that actually work
  • Warning signs + mental health red flags
View Social Media Guide
